# District Admin — your SlateSMS dashboard (easy guide)

This page is the **end-user** version. A more technical list of screens and permissions is in [`district-admin.md`](../district-admin.md).

---

## At a glance

This role uses the **District Admin** dashboard to complete school (or governance) work assigned to this role. Your **exact** screens may depend on what your school enables.

---

## Sign in and open the right place

- Use the **link and tenant code** your administrator gave you. Governance dashboards use the same app pattern, but the segment in the URL is your **state, district, or zone** (not a school).
- If you are unsure which URL to use, ask your **state/district/zone** administrator or IT contact.

- If you see **Unauthorized**, your user’s role in the database or session may not match this route (ask **system admin** or **school admin** to verify the account).


---

Use the menu in the order that matches *your* school’s process (your admin may give you a checklist).

---

## Common questions

- **I see "Unauthorized."** Your account may not be assigned to this **role** or the correct **school or tenant**, or you used a bookmark from another person. Sign out, sign in again, and if it still fails ask **school admin** or **IT / system admin**.
- **A menu item is missing.** Some modules are optional. Your **permissions** may also hide an item. Ask an administrator before assuming it is a bug.
- **Numbers or names look wrong.** Check **date**, **academic year**, and **class or section** filters, then check with the team that creates the data (for example, registrar, fee office, or school admin).
- **I am state, district, or zone staff and I do not see a school screen.** That can be normal: governance is scoped to your level. If you *should* have school access, ask your governance lead or IT to confirm your **role and tenant**.
